Write a program to find mean,variance and standard deviation.

<h3>Input Format</h3>
<pre>
	The first line of input specifies the number of test cases, t.
	Each test case consists of the length of the array, n, followed by n integers.
</pre>

<h3>Output Format</h3>
<pre>
	Print the mean,variance and standard deviation with a precision of 2 decimal places for each test case on a single line.
</pre>

<h3>Example Input</h3>
<pre>
	2
	7
	2 3 1 4 5 7 6
	6
	12 33 2 14 8 1
</pre>

<h3>Example Output</h3>
<pre>
	4.00 4.00 2.00
	11.67 113.56 10.66
</pre>
